11 Ways to reduce your gas bill

Most homes in the UK use gas for heating, hot water and/or cooking all year round, and while gas bills are likely to be higher in winter, there are steps you can take to reduce your gas bills and save money at any time. One of the most effective ways to reduce gas bills is to replace an old boiler with a new, energy efficient combi boiler, but there is upfront cost involved in a boiler replacement. We understand that you may not be able to finance a new boiler therefore we have listed some ways to lower your gas bill.
