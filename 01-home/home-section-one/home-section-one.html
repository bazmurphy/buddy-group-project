Installing solar panels on your roof could save you £455 per year

The Energy Saving Trust estimate that you can save around £455 per year by installing solar electricity panels on your roof. These panels capture the sun’s energy and convert it into electricity that you can use in your home. Sunlight is free, so once you’ve paid for the initial installation, you can generate your own renewable electricity and reduce your electricity costs for the year.

Dressing for the cold weather could save you up to £320 per year.

A light jumper adds around 3°C of warmth and a thicker one adds around 4°C. The best way to prevent yourself from needlessly adjusting your thermostat is to ensure you’re dressing appropriately during the winter. While it may sound obvious, by investing in a good pair of merino wool socks, a heavy seater, or a fleece bathrobe, you may save yourself a great deal of money on your gas bill per year.
