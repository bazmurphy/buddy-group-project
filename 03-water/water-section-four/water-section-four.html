Water Help

Don't worry if you are struggling with your water bill...

Every water company in England and Wales offers affordability support schemes and water efficiency measures.

Your water company may be able to:

  • Spread the cost of your bill.
  • Check if you qualify for a reduced bill.
  • Give you the option to pay weekly through your benefits - WaterDirect.
  • Help you apply for debt support schemes.
  • Give details of organisations that can give you free debt advice.
  • Pause debt alerts while you get expert advice.

Get help from your Local Council

Your local authority may also be able to support you with your energy issue directly through the Household Support Fund (in England) or Winter Fuel Support Scheme (in Wales) or point you towards local services in your area who can help you.

The Household Support Fund provides funding to help people who are struggling to afford food and utility bills (heating, cooking, lighting) and water (including drinking, washing, cooking, central heating, sewerage and sanitary purposes).

Contact your local council here https://www.gov.uk/find-local-council
